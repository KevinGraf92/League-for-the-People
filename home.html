League for The People

The League for the People is a fantast fotball league started with the innagural season in 2012.
What started as a way for for us to have a friendly competion each year, has now turned into a winner take all, no mercy battle for the top spot.
Competion keeps us honest, bring us closer, and allows us to unlock our competive nature.
Cheers to all and to another 10 years!
